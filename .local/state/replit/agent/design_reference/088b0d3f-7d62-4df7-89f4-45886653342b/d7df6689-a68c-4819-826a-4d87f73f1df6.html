Sikarni

Traditional Nepali sweet dessert made with thick milk, cardamom powder, and assorted nuts. Perfect for festivals and special occasions.

Cook Time
15 min
Servings
4
Cost/Serve
$3.50
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp mixed nuts (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp pistachios

Nutrition Facts

Calories 220
Protein 8g
Carbs 18g
Fat 12g

Instructions

1

Heat the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat. Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes until it reduces slightly.

2

Add sugar and cardamom powder to the milk. Stir well and let it cook for another 5 minutes until the sugar completely dissolves.

3

Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature. Then refrigerate for at least 2 hours until chilled.

4

Before serving, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Gently fold it into the chilled milk mixture.

5

Serve in individual bowls and garnish with chopped nuts and pistachios. Enjoy this traditional Nepali dessert chilled!

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy, affordable, and delicious meals, especially students who are away from home. Our mission is to make cooking accessible and enjoyable for international students and beginners.

Founded by students, for students, we understand the challenges of cooking on a budget, limited time, and minimal kitchen equipment. Every recipe is tested and designed with these constraints in mind.
