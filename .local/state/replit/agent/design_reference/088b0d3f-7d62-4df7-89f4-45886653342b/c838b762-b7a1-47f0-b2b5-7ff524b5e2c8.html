Momo

Traditional Nepali dumplings with savory filling

4
Servings
45
Minutes
$5.50
Cost
Easy
Difficulty
Non-Vegetarian Main Dish

Ingredients

  • Ground meat 500g
  • All-purpose flour 2 cups
  • Onion 1 medium
  • Ginger-garlic paste 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce 2 tbsp
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

1

Prepare the dough by mixing flour with water and a pinch of salt. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.

2

Mix ground meat with chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.

3

Roll the dough into small circles and place filling in the center.

4

Fold and seal the edges to form dumpling shapes.

5

Steam for 15-20 minutes until cooked through. Serve hot with tomato chutney.

Nutrition Facts

Calories 285 kcal
Protein 18g
Carbs 28g
Fat 12g

Cooking Tips

  • • Don't overfill the dumplings
  • • Seal edges tightly to prevent opening
  • • Steam on high heat for best results
  • • Serve immediately while hot

About Sathi Ko Recipe

Bringing authentic Nepali flavors to international students and food enthusiasts around the world.

Our Mission

At Sathi Ko Recipe, we believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's a bridge that connects us to our heritage, our memories, and our loved ones. Our mission is to make authentic Nepali cuisine accessible to everyone, especially international students who are far from home and seeking comfort in familiar flavors.

We understand the challenges of cooking in a new country with limited resources and unfamiliar ingredients. That's why our recipes are carefully crafted to be simple, budget-friendly, and adaptable to ingredients available in international markets.

Our Story

Founded by a group of Nepali students studying abroad, Sathi Ko Recipe was born from a simple desire: to recreate the comforting flavors of home in our tiny student apartments.

We spent countless hours experimenting in our dorm kitchens, calling our grandmothers for secret ingredients, and adapting traditional recipes to work with limited equipment and budgets.

What started as a personal journey soon became a community mission. We realized that many international students shared our struggle, and thus Sathi Ko Recipe was born—a platform where tradition meets practicality.
