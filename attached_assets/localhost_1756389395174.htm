    About Sathi Ko Recipe

    We're passionate about helping international students and cooking beginners create delicious, affordable meals that remind them of home while exploring new flavors.

    Our Mission

    Born from the challenges faced by international students living away from home, Sathi Ko Recipe bridges the gap between homesickness and culinary adventure. We believe that good food shouldn't be complicated or expensive.

    Our carefully curated recipes focus on simple ingredients, clear instructions, and budget-friendly approaches that make cooking accessible to everyone, regardless of their culinary background.
